=== antenna output temperature

physical temperature of correctional impedance that delivers to the receiver the same noise power as the antenna collects

NOTE: This includes two terms: the noise coming from the environment attenuated by the antenna Ohmic efficiency and the thermal noise added by the antenna Ohmic losses.
